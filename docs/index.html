Alert, Report and raise awareness about violence

Report acts of violence, alert your friends during a crisis and visualize raw and hard data about a real issue

Coming soon to Google Play and iOS App Store!

Temis was born as part of Product Hunt's global hackaton during November 2017.

Features

Alert

Send an alert to your closest contacts and friends on social networks when you feel in danger.

Report

When the danger has passed, create a report telling everyone about your experience.

Stats

Visualize what our user network has been reporting on a day-to-day basis that creates awareness.

Help others

When another user is in danger, all of the close-by users will get an alert, so you can go help them.

Temis is built as an open source project.
